    Firts day of the PGC went by fast. I had time to check the Indie game area and met old friends and made some new ones. Also during the first day I talked a lot about studying game programming in Xamk and how my thesis has gone.

    About the Game Job Fair

    I attended Games Job Fair Spring 2024 in Helsinki. During Games Job Fair I listened talks about game industry in finland and where to look for a job. There was also a possibility to meet industry professionals and to get their oppinion on your CV. I took the opportunity to get my CV and LinkedIn checkd by a professional and after the event I made changes to both based on the feedback I got during the meeting.
