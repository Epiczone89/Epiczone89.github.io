    FGJ 2023 Grandmas Cottage

    Link to Itch.io page where you can download the game: FGJ23 Grandma's Cottage

    1. Description

    Grandmas cottage was a game we did on Finnish Game Jam 2023. We had a team of 5. Theme of the jam was Roots. Game was finnished in time. I was also volunteering in the jam.

    2. Description of functions

    It was my first time making audio settings in game project. Beacause I it was my first time making audio settings most of the time went to fix problems that occured. Most time consuming problem I got was to get the slider to save audio volume in playerprefs and after that to make the change in audiomixer.

    Settings manager controlls all audio settings in the project. Plan was to add more settings but we didn't have enough time for that. Settings manager saves music volume in playerprefs and volume is contolled by slider. Audio can be adjusted in mainmenu and in the game using volume slider.

    Load prefs handels music volume in the game. This gives option to adjust game volume in mainmenu and also in game

    My role

    I made the MainMenu and settings UI and settings scripts.
