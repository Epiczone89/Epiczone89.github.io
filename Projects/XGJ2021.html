    XGJ21 Fight,Flight or Fljort

    Link to Itch.io page where you can download the game: XGJ21 Fight Flight or Fljort

    1. Description

    Fight, Flight or Fljort was a game we did in Xamk Game Jam 21. We had a team of six, Harto Kuukka, Joel Heinonen, Kai Hyvönen, Iida Nisula, Laura Malinen ja Jesse Rinneranta. Theme of the jam was Arcade. Game was almost finnished in time of the jam. Few functionality was left out because we run out of time. We planned the game very quickly so that time would not be wasted on it. The plan went trough the main functionalities of the game and also went trough who start doing what. Work was divided so that Laura and Iida did 3D modeling, Kai designed and coded the game field, Harto and Joel did the main coding, Joel also did consept art. I mainly made and coded the UI myself and helped with coding. The schedule for the jams was 45 hours and our team got at least a little design flaw in not taking into account the fact that the cloks were moved to summer time in the middle of the jams.

    2. Description of functions

    The game has three playble characters to choose from and they have different strengths and weaknesses. The game is a dungeon crawler and also has the ability to summon assistants that give more power to fights in the game's combats. In the combats you have opportunity to do three things either fight the enemy, escape from the enemy or flirt with the enemy to get past him. In each room of the game there is a possibility to collide with an enemy or a loot where you can get coins or health potions,

    3. Problems and solutions

    For myself there was no other problem in project other than its setup, witch took an hour. We used Unity collab, wich allows 5 people to join the project. We solved this by using same Unity ID between two people. Also adding people to that Unity Collab project also became a problem. This too was resolved fairly easily and painlessly.
